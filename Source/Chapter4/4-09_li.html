Specialty Pizzas

  • Barbecue Chicken Pizza

    This hearty American departure from Italian tradition is one of our most popular pizzas.

    • Spicy barbecue sauce.
    • Chunks of mesquite grilled chicken.
    • Blend of three cheeses:
      • Mozzarella
      • Monterey Jack
      • Smoked Gouda
    • Thin-sliced red onion.
    • Roasted red peppers.
